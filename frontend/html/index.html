Scientific Context

Understanding the Science

Respirable Fine Particles

PM 2.5

Particles less than 2.5 micrometers in diameter. These are significant health risks as they can bypass the nose and throat to enter the lungs and bloodstream.

Inhalable Coarse Particles

PM 10

Larger particles typically found near roadways and dusty industries. While less mobile than PM2.5, they contribute significantly to chronic respiratory inflammation.
